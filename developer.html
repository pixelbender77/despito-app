About the developer

The developer of “Despito” is a passionate programmer and nutrition enthusiast who wants to make healthy eating easier and more accessible for everyone.
She has a background in computer science and has been working on various projects related to artificial intelligence, web development, and data analysis. She created “Despito” as a personal project to combine her interests and skills, and to share her knowledge and experience with others.
She hopes that “Despito” will help users improve their health and wellness, and inspire them to learn more about nutrition and its impact on their lives.
